Our Mission

At New Moon Goat Rescue, we are dedicated to saving and rehabilitating goats in need throughout Washington State. Our mission is to provide a safe haven where rescued goats receive love, care, and the necessary medical attention.

Latest Rescue

Recently, our team rescued a small herd from a neglected farm. Their transformation under our care is a true testament to the resilience of these amazing animals.
